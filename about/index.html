About

Who am I#

I’m a software engineer for about 10 years, primarily at startups. I thrive in fast paced environment where accountability and ownership are high.

Outside of work, I enjoy drawing and painting with my daughter(mostly silly things), reading books, and feeding my board game obsession.

What I do#

My experience is majorly working with microservices, databases and building the systems behind products.

I love to dig deep into multiple technologies and infrastructure components to find the optimal balance of scale, cost, and operational overhead. My core expertise is in Node.js and TypeScript, backed by years of hands-on work with AWS and Kubernetes-giving me a strong grasp of both application code and the infrastructure that runs it. Following are some of the works that I have done:

  • At Nuralogix, I built multi-region infrastructure which enabled me to re-architect the application layer for scale, making sure that we all expand well while minimizing operational overhead and improving debugging, accessibility, and extensibility for development teams.

  • At Niyo, I led the core platform that powered the company’s first direct-to-consumer banking integrations with IDFC and Equitas. Beyond the initial launch, I focused on scaling the product and growing the team’s technical confidence to enable team to independently drive feature discussions and own implementation decisions.
